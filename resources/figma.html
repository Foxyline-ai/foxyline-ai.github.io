Brand Identity System

A minimalist geometric identity for a premium web design agency. Sophisticated, human-first, and timeless.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

Quiet luxury through geometric minimalism

The Foxyline identity embodies refined simplicity. The geometric fox mark is built from clean angular shapes and negative space—a subtle reference that suggests cleverness and agility without literal representation.

Every element serves a purpose. The warm, natural palette creates approachability within premium positioning. The geometric sans-serif typography balances precision with human warmth. This is a brand that feels boutique, sophisticated, and timeless.
